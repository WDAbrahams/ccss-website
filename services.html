Services

A full hospitality offering - from venue operations to catering to bespoke business tourism programmes.

What we do

Six pillars of the CCSS practice.

Each service stands on its own - and they combine seamlessly when an engagement calls for an end-to-end approach.

Hospitality Management

A complete hospitality offering at our managed venues - Springbok Guest Farm and Platfontein Lodge & Conference Centre - covering front-of-house, accommodation, guest experience and the operational systems that keep everything running smoothly.

Conferencing & Events

Local and international conferences, presidential visits, exhibitions, awards ceremonies and high-profile events - planned, produced and delivered end-to-end. From programme design and AV to registration and on-the-day choreography, we treat every event as a marketing tool that speaks directly to its audience.

Facility & Venue Operations

CCSS manages two venues in the Northern Cape - Springbok Guest Farm and Platfontein Lodge & Conference Centre. Day-to-day operations, maintenance, vendor coordination and logistics: the operational backbone behind every great guest experience we host.

Catering

Seasonal, locally inspired menus designed for scale - corporate breakfasts, working lunches, gala dinners, weddings and bespoke catering for private functions. Run from the CCSS kitchen and tailored to every occasion we host.

Business Tourism Solutions

Curated programmes for visiting government delegations, corporate groups and international guests - accommodation at our managed venues, transfers, group itineraries, and the on-the-ground experiences that turn a working trip into a memorable visit to the Northern Cape.

Weddings

Beautifully planned wedding days - intimate ceremonies, traditional celebrations and large receptions. Venue, florals, decor, catering and on-the-day coordination, handled by one team and shaped around your dream and vision down to the smallest detail.
